SERVICES

ARCHITECTURAL DESIGNING

The unique character of a building is expressed by the distinctive style of its exterior design. Our experts know the importance of respecting your architectural requirements and technical constraints. They listen carefully to understand your needs in style, technology, environment, maintenance and budget. Their know-how and experience means they will deliver a tailor-made, optimised solution that combines aesthetics with technical performance, and will last for decades.

STRUCTURAL DESIGNING

We render Structural Engineering and Project Management services for High Rise Buildings, Residential Buildings, Apartments, Educational Institutions, Hospitals, Hotels, Malls, Multiplex, Theaters, Industries, PEB’s (Pre Engineered Buildings) and General Structures. Our structural engineering services includes Foundation plan drawings, Drawings for shallow, raft and pile foundations design and drawing, RCC joint, slab details, roof truss and joint details.

INTERIOR DESIGNING

The demands on modern façade systems go beyond aesthetics; they must be energy efficient and deal with drainage, heat, cold, light and noise. These requirements are essential for creating a pleasant indoor ambiance. The cladding must also allow a simple and smooth installation, easy access and exchangeability to ensure maintenance is cost-effective.

LANDSCAPE DESIGNING

Landscape Design More Art Than Science

Since plants are at the heart of landscape design, knowledge of the science of horticulture is one of its critical components. The field is, however, more of an art than a science. Some decisions will be based not on hard fact, but on personal tastes, intuition, or current consensus.
